Controls
for the n-slice800
Total number of validators across the entire Avalanche network. This is what drives the global fee pressure in ACP-77 and the network factor in the Gaussian model.
for the V-slice10
How many validators your specific L1 runs. This is where the decentralization discount kicks in. More validators means a lower per-validator fee, but a higher total.
above target30
How many consecutive days the network has stayed above the 10k validator target. ACP-77 is path-dependent: the longer the pressure lasts, the higher it compounds. Only affects the ACP-77 curve when V is set above 10,000.
